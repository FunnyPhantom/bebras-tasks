Escape Game

Drag onetwofour players to the entrance, to build a team able to go through all the rooms and exit.

You need to follow the signs. For example, a player must wear a scarf to go through a corridor that has a scarf sign.

For a team to go through a room, every player must go through a different corridor.

A player may use a corridor that has two entrances if they can go through one of them.
